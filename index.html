Bio

Tom is a producer, currently in his final year of studies at ACM Guildford where he has worked with and been taught by many industry professionals such as John Gallen (Queen, Simply Red), Adrian “Stretch” Shortman (Eric Clapton, Sugababes) and many more.

Tom produces across multiple genres from pop to trap and is currently producing his first professional EP, scheduled for release later in 2019. As well as producing for himself, He is also working on collaborations with other producers and artists, offering his skills to help produce, mix and master multiple genres of work. Toms work does not end with producing songs, he has also worked on film music and scoring for short films such as “Infinite scope” and “about the little things”

Aside from Toms production, he also regularly DJ’s at clubs in the south of England and has his first international gig playing a set at Spring Break festival in Amsterdam. Tom can mix a multitude of genres from Drum and Bass to Club classics but predominately spins Tech House.
