Bio

Tom is a producer, recently graduating with a first class honours degree from ACM Guildford where he has worked with and been taught by many industry professionals such as John Gallen (Queen, Simply Red), Adrian “Stretch” Shortman (Eric Clapton, Sugababes) and many more.

Tom produces across multiple genres from pop to trap and is currently working as a artist producing house music. As well as producing for himself, He is also working on collaborations with other producers and artists, offering his skills to help produce, mix and master multiple genres of work. Toms work does not end with producing songs, he has also worked on film music and scoring for short films such as “Infinite scope” and “about the little things”

Aside from Toms production, he also regularly DJ’s at clubs in the south of England and featured in two major eurpoen festival lineups, Spring break Amsterdam and Budafest. Tom can mix a multitude of genres from disco to Club classics but predominately spins Tech House.
